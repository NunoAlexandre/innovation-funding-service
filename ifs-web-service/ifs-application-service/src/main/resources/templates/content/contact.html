Contact us

We can provide you with information and advice on Innovate UK competitions or help and technical support on using the Innovation Funding Service.

Contact our Customer Support Service

Phone

Call 0300 321 4357 to speak to an adviser (8.30am to 5.30pm, 5 days a week). Calls are free from landlines and most mobile numbers. Find out more about call charges and freephone numbers.

Email

Your question or query to support@innovateuk.gov.uk and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Assistance

If you need help completing your application, we can offer telephone, web conference or face to face assistance.

Please note that we run a competitive process and must treat all applications equally. This means we are unable to provide or advise on the actual content of your application.

Call 0300 321 4357 to make an appointment with an adviser.
