10. How does financial support from Innovate UK and its funding partners add value?

Although steps are under way to get this project off the ground, investment by Innovate UK will stimulate Industry leads to supply additional resource funding and use of their facilities as required to advance the progress of this project. UK Investment will ensure that we are able to retain the onward manufacture internally instead of needing to create international deals taking work and profit out of the country.
