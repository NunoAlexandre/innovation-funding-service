Invitation to assess 'Technology inspired'

You are invited to assess the competition 'Technology Inspired'.

15 April 2017 to 25 April 2017: Assessment period

This is the given timescale for reading, scoring, providing written comments and submitting your assessments in our online service. Each assessor could receive up to 10 proposals to assess, however this number could be less dependent on how many applications have been submitted. Our assessment fee for this competition is £100 per application.

The assessor webinar briefing will last one hour, taking place at 12 noon on 21 March.

Competition details: Technology inspired

Innovate UK is to invest up to £3 million in feasibility studies to stimulate innovation across four enabling technology areas that underpin future UK growth: advanced materials, biosciences, electronics, sensors and photonics, and information and communication technology (ICT).

See competition brief

You should reject this invitation if:

  • you are unavailable for the assessment period dates
  • you are unable to meet the assessment deadlines
  • this competition is not in your area of expertise
  • you have a direct conflict of interest, for example intending to apply to this competition yourself
