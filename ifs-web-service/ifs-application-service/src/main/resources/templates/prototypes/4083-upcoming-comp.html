Upcoming competition 'Technology inspired'

You have agreed to be an assessor for the upcoming competition 'Technology Inspired'

15 to 25 April: Assessment Period

Assessment period for reading, scoring, providing written comments and submitting to our online service. Each assessor could receive up to 10 applications to assess. This depends on the application response. Our assessment fee for this compeition is £100 per application

The assessor briefing is mandatory. It will consist of a one hour webinar on 21 March 2017.

Competition Details: Technology Inspired

Innovate UK is to invest up to £3 million in feasibility studies to stimulate innovation across four enabling technology areas that underpin future UK growth: advanced materials; biosciences; electronics, sensors and photonics; and information and communication technology (ICT).
