As the lead partner in this collaboration you must supply the additional documents to support your grant.

These documents are:

  • Collaboration agreement
  • Exploitation plan

These documents must be discussed and agreed with the other members of your collaboration, and when uploaded will become available for them to view.

Further details on what is required from these documents is available below.

Collaboration agreement

Collaboration agreement

Further details on what is required from these documents is available below.

What should I include in the collaboration agreement?

This is an agreement between project partners and will cover how the project will operate. It will also include details of how you will exploit the results of the project. Please allow enough time to complete this process before your project start date.

The following partners need to sign this collaboration agreement:

  • Aptrans Ltd
  • Biotech Corp
  • University of Cardiff

An example of a collaboration agreement can be found on the Lambert Agreement website.

The uploaded collaboration agreement must:

  • be in a Portable Document Format (.pdf)
  • be legible at 100% magnification
  • be less than 1mb in file size

Exploitation plan

Exploitation plan

The aim of the plan is to confirm the business case for the project and describe the partner activities towards exploitation of the results of the project.

What should I include in the exploitation plan?

The exploitation plan must include:

  • business Case including partners capability to develop and exploit the technology developed
  • market and commercial opportunities of the expected deliverables
  • commercial returns expected from the project, together with timescales
  • potential impact of the project for those outside the consortium
  • overall Business Plan
  • dissemination Activities during the project

A framework of the exploitation plan is available for download

The uploaded exploitation plan must:

  • be in a Portable Document Format (.pdf)
  • be legible at 100% magnification
  • be less than 1mb in file size
