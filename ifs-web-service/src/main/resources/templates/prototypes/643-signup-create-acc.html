Competition eligibility

To begin a new application for the competition 'Technology Inspired' you must meet the following criteria

  • Create this application as the lead partner of the consortium

    If you are not the lead, please contact your lead partner to create the application

  • Lead this application as a Business

    Applications into this competition must be lead by a business

  • Be based in the UK

    Non UK organisations are not eligible to recieve funding from Innovate UK
